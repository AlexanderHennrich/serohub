Introduction

The SeroHub is a platform to establish an open research forum and information environment, focusing on knowledge sharing, creating and fostering activities. Furthermore, SeroHub provides a general overview of research activities about seroprevalence studies.

Overview Seroprevalence Studies

The plattform will provide an overview of all participating studies, about their: * Study Population, * Time of sampling, * Total population in the area, * Research questions, * Diagnostic test, * When is the start of sampling expected, * When are results expected and * Funding source.

Later stages

All contributing researcher will get the opportiunity to submit anonymizied aggregated data and perfrom a meta-analysis with the data that other researchers share.

The general webpage concept is split into the general public site and the service environment only participating researcher can use.
